            JITLibExtensions
            Extension

            popular extensions of the Just-in-time Library

            JITLibExtensions include a wide range of Classes built with and for JITLib objects.

            NodeProxy / Ndef extensions

            • ProxyChain - play ordered synth and filter functions flexibly in one proxy
            • MasterFX - a configurable chain of effects on the main outputs of a server
            • NPVoicer, NPVoicerL - allocate voices within an Ndef
            • ParamGui, ProxyChainGui, MasterFXGui - associated guis

            General objects

            • BusSynthDefs - interpolate between values on buses
            • Halo - associate a halo of meanings to any object - specs, tags, etc
            • BufEnvir - a referentially transparent buffer environment
            • StoredList - store and recall named lists of settings

            Presets

            • ProxyPreset - store and morph between settings of JITLib Proxies.
            • PdefPreset, TdefPreset - for Pdefs and Tdefs
            • NodeProxyPreset, NdefPreset - ... for NodeProxy/Ndef ...
            • ProxyChainPreset - ... for ProxyChain/MasterFX ...
            • ProxyPresetGui, TdefPresetGui, PdefPresetGui, NdefPresetGui - the gui classes

            Recording, Mixing & Monitor

            • ProxySubmix
            • ProxyMeter, PrePostView
            • RecNodeProxy, RecordProxyMixer2

            Networking

            These are mostly experimental; several have good replacements in the Utopia quark.

            • BroadcastServer
            • EnvironmentRedirect
            • EnvirDispatch, Public
            • HistoryDispatch
            • Client, LocalClient
            • ClientFunc, ResponderClientFunc
