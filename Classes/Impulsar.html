      Description

      Triggers of different lengths and rates have different sonic results when used as excitors or when used with UGens like Decay. This converts a control rate 'trig' to an 'audio' rate single sample pulse.

      K2A.ar(trig) would sound different, being a whole control period and has K2A interpolation.
